Community Relations

Community Relations informs the community about UCSF campus plans and activities, gathers community feedback about upcoming projects, and relays any concerns or suggestions to our campus decision makers.

Mission Bay and Dogpatch

Mission Bay and Dogpatch are critical neighborhoods to UCSF, and we’re well aware of the impact, both positive and negative, that UCSF has on this community. We try to lessen the impact of ongoing construction, work with neighborhood organizations to improve what it’s like to live and work here, and talk with neighbors about their concerns.

We also try to inform the community about ongoing resources that are available to them. There are farmer’s markets, movie screenings, restaurants, job fairs, medical services, lectures and more.
